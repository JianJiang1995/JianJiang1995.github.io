Jian Jiang (姜键)

Postdoctoral Researcher

Shanghai Jiao Tong University

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, working with Prof. Yutong Ban. I obtained my Ph.D. from King's College London (KCL) in 2024, supervised by Prof. Oya Celiktutan.

Research Interests

Large Language/Vision Models, Surgical AI, Parameter-Efficient Transfer Learning, Model Compression, Incremental/Continual Learning, Multimodal Learning, Computer Vision, Affective Computing, Model Fairness, Medical Image Analysis.

Education

  • Ph.D. in Deep Learning / Computer Vision, King's College London, 2019 – 2024
  • M.Sc. in Artificial Intelligence (Distinction), King's College London, 2018 – 2019
  • B.Sc. in Software Engineering, Wuhan University of Science and Technology, 2014 – 2018

News

Mar 2026 SurgΣ is released — a large-scale multimodal data and foundation model spectrum for surgical intelligence, covering 6 clinical specialties and over 5.98M conversations across 18 surgical tasks.
Mar 2026 Surg-R1 is released — a hierarchical reasoning surgical VLM achieving 57.7% Arena Score, outperforming GPT-5.1 (28.5%) and Gemini 3.0 Pro (29.8%) on surgical benchmarks.
Mar 2026 SCISSR is released — a scribble-promptable framework for interactive surgical scene segmentation, achieving 95.41% Dice on EndoVis 2018 and 96.30% Dice on CholecSeg8k.
Dec 2025 Systematic Evaluation and Guidelines for Segment Anything Model in Surgical Video Analysis is accepted by NPJ Digital Surgery.
2024 Joined Shanghai Jiao Tong University as a Postdoctoral Researcher.
2024 Paper on incremental bias mitigation accepted by IEEE Transactions on Affective Computing (IF: 9.6).
